About Us

Welcome to our association! Here you can learn more about who we are, our mission, and our team.

Our Mission

Oasis Association for Sustainable Development, established on November 17, 2013, in Hadd Al-Sawalem under the name “Al-Alia Association 2”, is passionate about environmental awareness and education on road safety and citizenship. Since its establishment, the association has worked to enhance awareness and sensitization to consolidate the values ​​of citizenship education socially, environmentally, religiously, and health-wise in educational institutions in the cities of Al-Salem, Nador, and Zagora, with the participation of civil activities and guardian bodies:

Sports Initiatives

In the sports field, the Oasis Association for Sustainable Development organized football courses for the first time in Al-Salem city, Berrechid Province, with the participation of 15 teams from different areas during the years 2015 and 2016.

Seminars and Conferences

The association organized scientific, political, social, and religious seminars, moderated by a group of professors who left their mark on Moroccan and Arab society, including:

  • The great journalist Abdel Salam Al-Azouzi, head of the Future Academy for Media and Communication in Rabat
  • Dr. Al-Hami’i, researcher Abd al-Ilah al-Shahed
  • Moulay Ahmed Kannoun, engineer, expert in dynastic lands, and head of the National Authority for Dynastic Communities in Rabat
  • Khadija Barakat, member of the International Federation of African Women

Community Support

In 2017, the association's name was changed to “Oasis Association for Sustainable Development”. Despite the name change, the association continued to diversify its activities and keep pace with school life effectively. The association’s interests also expanded to include royal publishing and making dynastic lands a lever for improving the conditions of rights holders and promoting investment in them, in accordance with new laws, and to strengthening the autonomy initiative of the Kingdom of Morocco over its southern provinces.

COVID-19 Response

During the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020 and 2021, the association focused on supporting families in need by distributing food aid and basic materials. Additionally, the association implemented awareness campaigns about preventing the virus and adhering to health procedures, such as wearing masks, social distancing, and sterilization. These efforts helped enhance community awareness and improve the population’s ability to confront the health crisis.

Earthquake Relief Efforts

Oasis Association for Sustainable Development demonstrated its spirit of solidarity and humanity by supporting the victims of the Al-Haouz earthquake. The association collected and distributed urgent aid, including food, blankets, medicines, and basic supplies, to ensure the necessary needs of the affected families were met. The association's efforts were distinguished by the integration and solidarity of its members and their dedication to achieving its goals, with significant support from Professor Laila Habch.
